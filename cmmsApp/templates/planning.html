Elevator Pitch

CARL Work Plans is the planning engine inside CARL Source that turns maintenance policy into actionable, scheduled work. Build time-based, meter-, and condition-based plans, simulate workloads, reserve skills and parts, and publish optimized technician calendars then execute via CARL Touch on Android/iOS with full traceability.
