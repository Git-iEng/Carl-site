About Us

About us – where vision meets execution!

iEngineering Australia is a technology-driven solutions provider specializing in engineering software, industrial automation, and digital transformation services. We partner with organizations across multiple industries to deliver innovative, scalable, and cost-effective solutions tailored to their operational needs. With a strong focus on reliability, customer-centricity, and sustainability, our mission is to empower businesses with tools that enhance efficiency, improve asset performance, and drive growth.

Our Mission

At iEngineering Australia, our mission is to enhance efficiency, ensure quality control, and deliver customer satisfaction through innovative engineering solutions. We are committed to global standards and sustainable solutions that create long-term value for our clients and communities.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a global leader in engineering solutions, driving industry leadership, innovation, and customer-centric growth. We aim to inspire through global influence, foster expansion and growth, and play an inspirational role in shaping the future of engineering.

Our Values

Our values guide everything we do: integrity & ethics, a strong commitment to quality, and diversity & inclusion. Safety, reliability, and continuous improvement are the cornerstones of our success, enabling us to deliver trusted and innovative solutions worldwide.

Our Journey

Founded in 2013, iEngineering Australia has rapidly grown into a trusted technology partner delivering innovative solutions in asset management, IoT, cloud platforms, and digital transformation. From our beginnings as a small engineering services provider, we have expanded our expertise across industries such as utilities, transport, energy, manufacturing, and public infrastructure. Today, we are proud to support organizations across Australia and the Asia-Pacific region with smart, scalable, and future-ready technology solutions.

Where We Operate

iEngineering Australia operates across Australia, Fiji, and India, delivering reliable technology and asset management solutions to organizations of all sizes—from local enterprises to large multinational companies.
